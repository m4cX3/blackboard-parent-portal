Our Purpose

We aim to bridge the gap between parents and their child's academic journey by providing an easy-to-use platform for monitoring courses and progress through Blackboard Ultra.

Easy Access to Academic Progress

Parents can easily sign up and access their child's course details and grades with a user-friendly and intuitive interface designed for secure and quick navigation.

Parent Registration

Parents can easily register by providing basic information as well as their child's name or student ID. This ensures that the system links the correct parent to the correct student, creating a secure and effective connection.

Automatic Observer Account Generation

Once approved, the system generates a Blackboard Ultra observer account for the parent, automatically linking them to their child's courses. This simplifies the process for both parents and administrators.

How does it work?

Discover how we empower parents with the tools they need to stay engaged in their child's academic journey.

Admin Approval

Once a parent registers, an admin can review the application, approve it, and trigger the system to automatically generate a Blackboard Ultra observer account, ensuring only verified users gain access.

Email Notification & Access

Upon approval, the parent receives an email containing their observer account credentials and a direct link to Blackboard, allowing them to immediately log in and view their child’s academic progress.
